# Bajaj Ghabbour — Digital Advertising Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1–2)

### 1.1 Google Business Profile
- Claim/verify "Bajaj Ghabbour" on Google Maps
- Upload shop photos, logo, opening hours
- Add service categories: Motorcycle Repair, Motorcycle Dealer, Auto Parts
- Enable messaging & appointment booking links

### 1.2 Social Media Pages
| Platform | Purpose | Content Plan |
|---|---|---|
| **Facebook** | Community & events | 3 posts/week: tips, offers, behind-scenes |
| **Instagram** | Visual showcase | Reels of 3D bike, before/after repairs, showroom |
| **TikTok** | Viral reach | Short clips: engine sounds, mechanic skills, satisfied customers |
| **WhatsApp Business** | Direct communication | Auto-reply menu, catalog, quick replies |

### 1.3 Website SEO Basics
- Meta title: "Bajaj Ghabbour | Authorized Bajaj Service Center Cairo"
- Meta description with keywords: Bajaj Boxer repair, genuine parts, Cairo
- Add structured data (LocalBusiness schema)
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console

---

## Phase 2: Content Engine (Week 3–6)

### 2.1 Content Pillars
1. **Educational** — "How to check your oil", "When to service your Boxer"
2. **Social Proof** — Customer testimonials, before/after repairs
3. **Promotional** — Seasonal offers, spare parts discounts
4. **Behind the Scenes** — Mechanics at work, new stock arrivals

### 2.2 Video Content Ideas
| Idea | Platform | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| 3D Model showcase | Instagram Reels, TikTok | 1/week |
| Mechanic tip (30s) | TikTok, Reels | 2/week |
| Customer testimonial | Facebook, Instagram | 1/week |
| New bike unboxing | All platforms | As available |

### 2.3 Paid Ads — Starter Budget ($200–500/month)

**Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
- Objective: Lead generation (WhatsApp/phone calls)
- Audience: Men 22–45, within 25km of Cairo, interests: motorcycles, Bajaj, commuting
- Creative: 3D model animation video + "Book Service Now" CTA

**Google Ads:**
- Search: "bajaj service cairo", "boxer 180 repair", "motorcycle parts egypt"
- Display: Remarketing to website visitors

---

## Phase 3: Conversion Optimization (Week 7–10)

### 3.1 Website Enhancements for Ads
- Add Facebook Pixel + Google Analytics 4
- Create landing page: `/service-offer` with limited-time discount
- Add WhatsApp click-to-chat floating button on all pages
- Implement Google Reviews widget on homepage

### 3.2 Email/SMS Follow-up
- Collect phone/email from bookings & contact form
- Automated SMS reminders: "Your bike is ready for pickup"
- Monthly newsletter: tips + offers

---

## Phase 4: Scale & Loyalty (Week 11–16)

### 4.1 Influencer Partnerships
- Partner with 3–5 Cairo motorcycle vloggers
- Offer free service in exchange for honest review video
- Cross-post to your channels

### 4.2 Referral Program
- "Refer a friend → both get 15% off next service"
- Track via unique QR code or phone number

### 4.3 Retargeting Funnel
| Stage | Action | Ad Type |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness | 3D video ad | Facebook/Instagram video |
| Interest | Visit website | Google Display remarketing |
| Consideration | Viewed services | Carousel of specific services |
| Action | Started booking | WhatsApp click-to-chat ad |
